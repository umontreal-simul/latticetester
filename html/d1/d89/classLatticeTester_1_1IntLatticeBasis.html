Detailed Description

template<typename Int, typename BasInt, typename BasIntVec, typename BasIntMat, typename Dbl, typename DblVec, typename RedDbl>
class LatticeTester::IntLatticeBasis< Int, BasInt, BasIntVec, BasIntMat, Dbl, DblVec, RedDbl >

This class represents a lattice and its basis and offers tools to do basic manipulations on lattice bases.

Each lattice is at least represented by a basis \(V\), a dimension and a norm. The basis will be contained in a matrix of type BasIntMat. The dimension \(d\) specifies that \(V\) contains \(d\) vectors in \(\mathbb{Z}^d\). The norm is one of the norm of NormType. Users can also precise a dual lattice \(W\) and the modulo m used for rescaling.

This class also has methods and attributes that can be used to store and compute the norms of the basis and dual basis vectors. It can also permute vectors in the basis or sort them by length and do the corresponding changes in the dual. It can also check of the dual \(W\) really is a dual basis to \(V\).
